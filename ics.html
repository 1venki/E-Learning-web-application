Course Name: Integrated computer systems

Description
ICS, which stands for Information and Communication Technology, encompasses a broad range of technologies, systems, and applications that facilitate the management, storage, retrieval, transmission, and manipulation of digital information. It plays a crucial role in various aspects of modern society, including business operations, communication, education, healthcare, entertainment.

What you will learn in this course?

  • Learning about computer components, peripherals, and their functions.
  • Studying network architectures, protocols, and communication technologies.
  • Configuring and troubleshooting computer networks, routers, switches, and firewalls.
  • Learning about cybersecurity threats, vulnerabilities, and attack vectors.
  • Using data analytics tools and techniques to analyze and visualize data.
